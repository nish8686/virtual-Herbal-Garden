ABOUT US


The Virtual Herbal Garden for AYUSH is a digital initiative designed to showcase India’s vast and valuable
heritage of medicinal plants. Rooted in the traditional knowledge systems of Ayurveda, Unani, Siddha, and
Homeopathy (AYUSH) , our platform makes centuries-old wisdom accessible to everyone through modern technology.

We believe that nature holds solutions for health, wellness, and sustainable living. Our mission is to create
an interactive, educational, and easy-to-use platform where users can explore detailed information about
medicinal plants—their identification, benefits, uses, and cultural significance.

Our platform features:

1) Interactive Plant Cards with authentic details, uses, and precautions.
2) Search and Filter Options to find plants by name, category, or health benefit.
3) Multilingual Support to reach audiences across India.

By bridging the gap between ancient herbal knowledge and modern digital tools, we aim to:

1) Educate the public on the benefits of natural remedies.
2) Support farmers, herbal product manufacturers, and AYUSH practitioners.
3) Preserve India’s biodiversity by encouraging awareness and cultivation of medicinal plants.

This project is not just an app — it’s a step towards reviving traditional health practices in a
way that fits the lifestyle of today’s generation.
